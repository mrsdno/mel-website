About

Maleha Arif, aka Mel, is a self-taught contemporary artist that paints on big sized canvas using acrylics. When she’s not creating art, Mel is a mother to a 10-year old boy, her biggest fan and motivation, and an educator.Family, friends and community are important to Mel and aspects of values important to them can be found in all projects she takes on.

Paintings

Mel had a first successful solo exhibition at World Art Dubai in March 2022 where she showcased 10 paintings. Mel’s paintings are a burst of bright, vibrant colors, hoping to spread positivity and good vibes to their new homes. A lot of Mel’s paintings are inspired by nature, women empowerment and her role as an educator. Music also plays an important role in the creation of the art pieces, from the sketches and composition phases, all the way to adding the finishing touches. takes on.
